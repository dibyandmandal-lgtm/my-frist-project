Our Story

Built By Students,
For Students.

We believe that education shouldn't be a solo journey. EduNotes was created to connect students across India, making high-quality study materials accessible, organized, and free for everyone.

Transforming How India Studies.

It started with a simple problem: finding reliable notes before exams was frustrating. Notes were scattered across WhatsApp groups, Google Drives, and physical notebooks. The quality was inconsistent, and brilliant insights from top students were often lost.

We built EduNotes to change that. By bringing everything into one centralized, community-driven platform, we've empowered thousands of students to learn collaboratively.

  • Democratizing access to quality education.
  • Recognizing and rewarding student contributors.
  • Fostering a collaborative academic environment.

Our Vision for 2026

To become the digital backbone of higher education in India, connecting over 500 universities and 1 million students. We're actively expanding our feature set to include AI-driven study paths, interactive quizzes, and real-time collaborative whiteboards.

Education is evolving, and so are we. Our commitment is entirely to the student community — ensuring nobody is left behind due to a lack of resources.
